# Scope — V1

**Step S002** · Boundary detail: [06-v1-v2-boundary.md](06-v1-v2-boundary.md)

## In scope

| Area | Includes |
|------|----------|
| Public (10 pages) | Home, About, Agencies, Services, Tariffs, Visite Technique, Booking, Tracking, News, Contact (+ FAQ section) |
| Admin | Dashboard, agencies, services, tariffs, bookings, document readiness, contacts, articles, FAQs, gallery, testimonials, users, settings, audit |
| Booking | Intake form · reference `GS-YEAR-SEQUENCE` · staff confirm by phone/WhatsApp |
| Tracking | Appointment + document status via reference + phone + plate |

### Locked structure

- FAQ on **Contact** only (not a separate page)  
- Procedure + preparation = **Visite Technique**  
- Accepté / Suspendu / Refusé = educational copy only  

### Public booking message

**FR:** Sélectionnez votre agence, votre service et votre créneau préféré. Notre équipe vous contactera par téléphone ou WhatsApp pour confirmer la disponibilité.  
**EN:** Select your preferred agency, service and time slot. Our team will contact you by phone or WhatsApp to confirm availability.

## Out of scope (V1)

Lane/machine tracking · WhatsApp/SMS APIs · online payment · customer accounts · fleet portal · certificate/QR · publishing live inspection results as booking statuses

## Launch blockers

Official tariffs · email spelling confirmed · logo/photos decision · soft launch with staff
